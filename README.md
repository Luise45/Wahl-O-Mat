## Student Election Voting Advice Application (Wahl-O-Mat)
A web-based voting advice application designed for student elections, helping voters compare their views with those of the candidates. Candidates can express their positions on predefined statements, while voters receive a personalized recommendation based on their responses.

### **Features** 
For Candidates
- Register and log in securely
- View a predefined list of student-relevant statements

For Voters
- Indicate agreement levels for each student-relevant statement
- Receive a personalized voting recommendation, typically as a sorted list of candidates
---
